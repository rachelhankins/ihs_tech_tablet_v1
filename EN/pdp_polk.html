Polk Audio Cinema Surround Sound System

Available exclusively to OnTech customers, the 38” Polk Audio Cinema Sound Bar, subwoofer and surround speakers are the perfect trio for your home theater. The bar is engineered to produce amazing sound and fit beautifully under modern TV sizes. It pairs perfectly with the wireless subwoofer, which plays down to 35 Hz, and the wireless surround speakers, which will bring full-range surround sound to your living room.

Product Features

Voice Adjust

Boosts voice frequencies without increasing background audio, for clear/crisp dialogue.

Night Mode

Lower the bass and increase Voice Adjust without raising the master volume — optimizing sound for watching while others are sleeping.

Optimized for Connection

The bar is Bluetooth-enabled and has ports for HDMI (ARC), Optical and Analog 3.5mm to support various ways of connecting your existing setup.

Surround Sound Ready

This sound bar supports Dolby Digital and Dolby Digital Plus.

The Ultimate Combination

Get more powerful, room-filling bass when you use the wireless adapter to sync the sound bar, subwoofer and rear speakers.
