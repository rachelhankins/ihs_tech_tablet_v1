Google Nest Wifi Router and Point

Nest Wifi blankets your whole home in fast, reliable Wi-Fi.¹ With up to 3,800 sq. ft. of coverage, it automatically updates itself to get new features and helps keep your network safe. One Nest Wifi router with one Nest Wifi point is strong enough to handle up to 200 connected devices, and fast enough to stream multiple 4K videos at a time. And Nest Wifi points have a speaker with the Google Assistant for extra help around the house.

Product Features

Coverage in every corner

The Nest wifi router and point work together to blanket your whole home in fast, reliable Wi-Fi. You can walk from room to room on a video call with an uninterrupted signal.¹

Parental peace of mind

Parental permissions let you set schedules to manage screen time, restrict certain kinds of adult content, and pause Wi-Fi to specific devices whenever you want.

Access point with built-in Google Assistant

The Nest Wifi point has Google Assistant built it. It allows you control smart home devices, play music, manage your Wi-Fi network, and more, using your voice.²

Need more coverage?

Nest Wifi is a scalable and flexible Wi-Fi system. If you need more coverage, add Nest Wifi points for an additional 1600 square feet of Wi-Fi coverage and it has a smart speaker with the Google Assistant.

Manage your connected home

Nest Wifi makes it easy to add, set up, and manage compatible connected devices in your network, all in the Google Home app³. Check network speeds, create a guest network, share passwords and more.

Manage your family's Internet use

Keep track of your family's screen time - pause the Internet on your kids' devices at bedtime or during homework hours.

¹Home size, materials and layout can affect how Wi-Fi signal travels. Larger homes or homes with thicker walls or long, narrow layouts may need extra Wifi points for full coverage. Strength and speed of signal will depend on your internet provider. ²Controlling certain devices and features in your home requires a compatible smart device. ³Minimum OS requirements are available at g.co/nestwifi/req.
